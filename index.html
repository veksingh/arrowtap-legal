Introduction

Welcome to ArrowTap ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile puzzle game ArrowTap ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name, email address, and profile photo. Guest users play anonymously with no personal information collected.
  • Game progress — Completed levels, star ratings, hint usage, streak count, and best scores. This is synced to Firebase Firestore to enable cloud save across devices.
  • Device information — Basic device data collected automatically by Firebase Analytics including device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Usage data — Which levels you play, session duration, and in-app actions, used to improve the game experience.
  • Purchase data — If you make in-app purchases, transaction records are processed by Google Play. We store only which products you have purchased (e.g. "remove ads", "hint pack") — no payment card details are stored by us.

How we use your information

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To display your name and progress within the app
  • To provide the daily challenge leaderboard
  • To restore your purchases if you reinstall the app
  • To improve gameplay and fix bugs using analytics data
  • To show relevant advertisements to free users via Google AdMob

Advertising (Google AdMob)

ArrowTap shows advertisements to free users via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data about you to serve personalised ads. This may include your device's advertising ID and usage data. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will not see any advertisements.

For more information about how Google uses data in advertising, visit policies.google.com/technologies/ads .

Firebase services

We use the following Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication — For Google Sign-In and anonymous guest accounts
  • Cloud Firestore — To store and sync your game progress and purchase history
  • Firebase Analytics — To understand how players use the app
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — To send push notifications (daily challenge reminders, streak alerts)

Firebase data is stored on Google's servers. You can review Google's privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy .

Data retention

We retain your game progress and account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Analytics data is retained for 14 months as per Firebase Analytics default settings.

Children's privacy

ArrowTap is rated for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, database, and analytics services
  • Google AdMob — for advertising (free users only)
  • Google Play — for processing in-app purchases

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • Play as a guest with no personal data collected

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase's built-in security rules to protect your data. Your game progress is protected by Firestore security rules that ensure only you can read and write your own data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
