Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your data. You can request deletion of your ArrowTap account and all associated data at any time. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

⚠ What gets deleted permanently

  • Your Google account link and profile information
  • All completed levels, star ratings, and best scores
  • Your daily challenge history and leaderboard entries
  • Your streak count and play history
  • All in-app purchases (hint packs, unlocked level packs)
  • Your cloud-saved progress across all devices

Note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel any active subscriptions or issue refunds for purchases. In-app purchases are governed by Google Play's refund policy.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

1
Open ArrowTap on your device
Make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.
2
Go to Settings
Tap the settings icon (⚙) on the home screen.
3
Scroll to Account section
Tap "Delete Account" and confirm when prompted. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, send a request to our support team. Include the email address associated with your ArrowTap account.

Send Deletion Request vivek172.singh@gmail.com
⏱ We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What data is retained after deletion

After your account is deleted, we retain anonymised, non-identifiable analytics data (such as aggregate level completion rates) for up to 14 months as required by our analytics provider. This data cannot be linked back to you.

All personally identifiable information — including your name, email, game progress, and purchase history — is permanently deleted within 30 days.
